Heather Barker is an Associate Professor of Mathematics and Statistics at Elon University. She teaches both statistics courses as well as math education classes to support K-12 preservice teachers. Her research focuses on 
  - best practices for preparing K-12 preservice teachers to teach statistics standards they will learn.
  - using social justice to teach statistics.
  - using data to empower students to tell stories.
  - using history topics to teach statistics.
